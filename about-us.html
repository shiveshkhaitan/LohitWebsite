'First Deserve, the Desire'. It is a chant that guides one through the darkening troubles of life. A war cry that has been cried many  a times on several occasions. Lohit House has become a family over the years  where one can find brotherhood and companionship.  Our House teaches the students to not believe in frivolous idea of an easy living but rather to face obstructions and mountains head on. In life , we get only as much as we deserve. Thus it goes, "First Deserve, Then Desire".
